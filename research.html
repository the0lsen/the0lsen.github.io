Theo Olsen Research

Research

I'm interested in protoplanetary disks, the physics and chemistry of brown dwarf atmospheres, and using observational data to learn how planets and substellar objects form and evolve.

I currently work with the BDNYC group at the American Museum of Natural History, contributing to the SEDA software package and studying brown dwarf atmospheres.
